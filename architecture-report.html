Architectural Change in Enterprise IT

Summary

This research paper examines the fundamental transformation of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) architectures in the context of Industry 4.0, with a specific geographical focus on the highly industrialized regions of Liechtenstein, Eastern Switzerland, and Western Austria (Vorarlberg, Tyrol). Faced with volatile markets and technological disruptions, established manufacturing companies are confronted with the dilemma of reconciling their historically grown, monolithic core systems ("Systems of Record") with the need for agile, data-driven innovations ("Systems of Differentiation").

1. Introduction: The Architectural Dilemma of Digital Agility

Corporate IT in the manufacturing industry is at a historic turning point. For decades, monolithic ERP systems formed the indispensable backbone of operational value creation. However, in an era characterized by hyper-competition, shortened product life cycles, and the demand for lot size 1, these former anchors of stability are increasingly perceived as a brake on innovation.
